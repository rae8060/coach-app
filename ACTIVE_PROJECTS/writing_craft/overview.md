## What We Analyzed
1. **Prose Techniques** - Onomatopoeia, rhythm, short sentences
2. **Foreshadowing** - Chekhov's Gun, retroactive setup
3. **Character Development** - Hero's Journey, mentor cycles
4. **Story Structure** - Three-act per book, chapter cliffhangers
5. **Themes** - Death, love, choice vs. fate, prejudice
6. **Dialogue** - Subtext, dialect, the unsaid
7. **Setting** - Places as characters
8. **Editorial Choices** - What to show vs. skip

## How to Use This
When Richard writes his book, reference these techniques:
- Use short sentences for impact moments
