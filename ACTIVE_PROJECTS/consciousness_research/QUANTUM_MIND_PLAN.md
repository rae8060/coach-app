## 🔬 PHASE 2: EXPERIMENTS (Week 3-4)
### Simple At-Home Tests

**Experiments to Try:**
1. **Double Slit at Home**
   - Buy: Laser pointer ($10) + double slit ($15) from Amazon
   - Document: What we see, how observation changes it

2. **Consciousness Journal**
   - Each morning: Write down 1 intention
   - Each evening: Record what happened
   - Track for 30 days, look for patterns

3. **Meditation Focus**
   - 10 min daily meditation
   - Focus on "intending" something specific
   - Document results

**Action Items:**
